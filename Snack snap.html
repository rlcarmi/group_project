About Snack Snap

Snack snap is a unique site that allows it's users to upload food pictures from restaurants and cafes around Brighton.
Whether you want to practice your food photography skills, or hunt for the most mouth-watering meals in your area, Snack Snap is the site for you.
To upload an image taken at a restaurant you've loved, find the restaurant on our map and select 'upload'.
Get snapping!

Our Story - Code First: Girls

We all wanted to learn more about technology and coding and applied to a Sussex Uni Code First: Girls Beginners course on January 2019.

Over 8 weeks, we learned about HTML, CSS, JavaScript and more about web devlopment together and have managed to create Snack Snap as our final project, thanks to Code First: Girls.

A special shoutout to Rosanna, Shile, Kate and Victoria for being so paitent and teaching us about web development!
