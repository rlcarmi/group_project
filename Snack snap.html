About Snack Snap

Snack snap is a unique site that allows it's users to upload food pictures from restaurants and cafes around Brighton.
Whether you want to practice your food photography skills, or hunt for the most mouth-watering meals in your area, Snack Snap is the site for you.
To upload an image taken at a restaurant you've loved, find the restaurant on our map and select 'upload'.
Get snapping!
